| [ADR-0279](0279-fr-regressor-v2-probabilistic.md) | Probabilistic head on top of the codec-aware `fr_regressor_v2` — deep-ensemble (N=5 v2 members under distinct seeds) plus opt-in normalised split-conformal calibration. Surfaces a calibrated prediction interval `(mu, sigma, lower, upper)` instead of v2's bare MOS scalar so the in-flight `vmaf-tune --quality-confidence 0.95` flag (consumer of [ADR-0237](0237-quality-aware-encode-automation.md)) can answer queries of the form "smallest CRF where the lower 95 % bound is ≥ 92". Ships [`ai/scripts/train_fr_regressor_v2_ensemble.py`](../../ai/scripts/train_fr_regressor_v2_ensemble.py) + [`ai/scripts/eval_probabilistic_proxy.py`](../../ai/scripts/eval_probabilistic_proxy.py) + manifest sidecar `model/tiny/fr_regressor_v2_ensemble_v1.json`. Each ensemble member is a stock `FRRegressor(num_codecs=NUM_CODECS)` exported as a separate two-input ONNX (matches LPIPS-Sq precedent in ADR-0040 / ADR-0041); the manifest pins member sha256s, feature standardisation, codec vocab, nominal coverage, and an optional `conformal_q_residual` quantile from a held-out calibration split. Inference rule is `mu ± q · σ` with `q = 1.96` (Gaussian) or the empirical conformal quantile (distribution-free coverage guarantee on exchangeable data, Vovk 2005 / Romano 2019). Smoke-only ship (synthetic 100-row corpus, 1 epoch / member); production training is gated on the multi-codec Phase A corpus and tracked as backlog item T7-FR-REGRESSOR-V2-PROBABILISTIC.
